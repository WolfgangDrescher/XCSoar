Geometric gradient to the arrival height above the best alternate landing
location. This is not adjusted for total energy.

Alternate 1 / Alternate 2 and their GR / AltD variants may either display the
best automatic alternate (AUTO, default) or a manually selected alternate chosen
from the alternates list during flight. Manual selections are runtime-only and
remain active until changed again or switched back to AUTO. The value is shown
in red when the glide computer marks the alternate as not achievable at the
current MacCready setting.  In AUTO mode, blue indicates final glide and grey
that further climb is required.  In MANUAL mode, light green indicates the
chosen alternate is on final glide; red is also used when the solution is
achievable but still below final glide (further climb needed).
