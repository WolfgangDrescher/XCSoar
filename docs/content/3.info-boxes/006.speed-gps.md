Ground speed measured by the GPS. The secondary value (in smaller font) shows
the head or tail wind component relative to TAS — negative means tail wind,
positive means head wind. In simulator mode (Touch-screen/PC only), the up/down
arrow keys adjust the speed and the left/right arrow keys turn the glider.
