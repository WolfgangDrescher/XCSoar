Pressure altitude for IGC logging purposes. Shows the device's IGC pressure
altitude when available, otherwise the ISA pressure altitude (1013.25 hPa, not
QNH-corrected). Does not show barometric AMSL or GPS height — use other altitude
InfoBoxes for navigation. An InfoBox dialogue with Simulator, Info, and Setup
panels is available.
