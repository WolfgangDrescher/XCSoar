Arrival altitude at the best alternate landing location relative to the safety
arrival height. The secondary value shows the waypoint name. Alternate InfoBoxes
may display the best automatic alternate (AUTO, default) or a manually selected
alternate; manual selections remain active until changed or switched back to
AUTO. In AUTO mode: blue indicates the alternate is on final glide, grey means
further climb is required, and red means not achievable at the current MacCready
setting. In MANUAL mode: light green indicates the chosen alternate is on final
glide, and red means the solution is achievable but still below final glide
(further climb needed). Clicking the InfoBox opens the alternate waypoint
details.
