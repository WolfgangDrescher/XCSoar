Name and bearing to the best alternate landing location. The secondary value
shows the waypoint name. Alternate InfoBoxes may display the best automatic
alternate (AUTO, default) or a manually selected alternate chosen from the
alternates list during flight; manual selections remain active until changed or
switched back to AUTO. In AUTO mode: blue indicates the alternate is on final
glide, grey means further climb is required, and red means not achievable at the
current MacCready setting. In MANUAL mode: light green indicates the chosen
alternate is on final glide, and red means the solution is achievable but still
below final glide (further climb needed). Clicking the InfoBox opens the
alternate waypoint details.
