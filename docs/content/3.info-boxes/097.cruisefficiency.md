Efficiency of cruise relative to the optimal MacCready speed-to-fly. A value of
100% means the cruise speed matches the MacCready optimal speed. Values below
100% indicate flying too slow; above 100% indicates flying too fast. Calculation
begins after the task is started. Clicking the InfoBox opens the cruise
efficiency analysis page.
