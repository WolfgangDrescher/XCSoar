Estimated turning circle diameter based on true airspeed and heading turn rate.
Requires airspeed data. Displays `---` when airspeed is unavailable, the turn
rate is less than 1°/s, or the estimated diameter exceeds 2 km. The secondary
value shows the estimated duration of one full circle in seconds.
