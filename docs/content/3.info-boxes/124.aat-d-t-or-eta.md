Combined InfoBox that shows different data depending on the task type. For AAT
tasks, the title shows "AAT delta time" and displays the difference between the
estimated task time and the minimum AAT time, with the secondary value showing
the ETA. For racing tasks, the title shows "Task arrival time" and displays the
estimated time of arrival at task completion.
